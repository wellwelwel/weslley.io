:::note Summary

<Side id="resume">

I will show how to report security issues, approaches to handling vulnerability reports, their corresponding fixes, and how to test them end-to-end.

I'll also cover resilience when maintaining a project that directly impacts millions of developers worldwide: a single bug could mean data loss or corrupted data at a global scale.

More than code, you'll learn about security, interoperability, resilience, and how open source can impact your company and your career.
